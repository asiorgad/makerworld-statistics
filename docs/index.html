📊 MakerStats

MakerWorld Statistics Display for ESP32

⚠️ Web Serial is not supported in this browser. Please use Chrome, Edge, or Opera on desktop.

Flash Firmware to Your ESP32

Your browser doesn't support Web Serial Serial access denied

Requirements

  • ESP32 board (ESP32, ESP32-C3, or ESP32-S3)
  • GC9A01 round TFT display (240x240)
  • Chrome, Edge, or Opera browser
  • USB cable connected to your ESP32

After Flashing - Setup Steps

⚠️ Important: Configure the Gist URL in the "Setup" tab BEFORE configuring WiFi!
1

Connect to MakerStats-Setup WiFi

After flashing, the device creates a WiFi access point. Connect to it from your phone or computer.

2

Go to "Setup" Tab First

In the captive portal, click on the "Setup" tab and enter your Gist URL:

https://gist.githubusercontent.com/YOUR_USERNAME/GIST_ID/raw/bambu.txt
3

Configure WiFi

Go to the "WiFi" tab, select your network, and enter the password.

4

Save & Enjoy!

The device will connect and start displaying your MakerWorld statistics.

Data Format

Your Gist file should contain space-separated values:

Name Username Followers Following Boosts Likes Downloads Prints

Example:

JohnDoe @johndoe 1.2k 50 234 567 890 123
